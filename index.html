What

SenseCamp is a Barcamp dedicated to social business and social entrepreneurship: It brings together social entrepreneurs, social business enthusiasts and others for one full day of talks, workshops and networking.

We want to promote social entrepreneurship, highlight local social projects and strengthen the Berlin based community of social innovators. The camp is the unique chance to share your knowledge and learn from others. We want to create a place where you can discover and exchange new ideas, insights and experiences. We do this in order to motivate people for social entrepreneurship!

In the end it's all about fun, knowledge and sharing! We hope you join us!

Who

SenseCamp is an open event designed and produced by an enthusiastic gang of Social Entrepreneurs. The core team is composed of MakeSense, BetterWorld Network, knowable.org and the Social Impact Lab.
